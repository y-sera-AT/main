About the tool

What is Y-SERA?

Y-SERA is a web-based assessment tool designed to identify youths' readiness to engage in social entrepreneurship. It assesses seven key dimensions that contribute to social entrepreneurship readiness, then maps each participant onto one of three readiness levels with tailored next steps.
